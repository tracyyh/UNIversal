Welcome to Montréal!

Montréal is the largest city in the Canadian province of Quebec. Named after Mount Royal, the triple-peaked hill located at its center, it is the second most populous city in Canada. It is home to a majority of French-speaking citizens, with its total population of 1.78 million. It is one of the largest french-speaking cities in the world. In Montréal, there are a total of 36 colleges in Montréal, 11 of those being universities.

Throughout its entire education system, there is a total of about 18,000 foreign/international students from over 150 countries. Around 35,000 international students enter Montréal a year, as it is ranked the best student city in North America. Montréal is hub for many immigrants in Canada, with its universal free healthcare, affordable housing, and inexpensive high quality education. As a result, the culture is rich and diverse, with a motley of people coming in from around the world.
