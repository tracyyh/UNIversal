About UNIversal

A lot of students who immigrate to a different country/continent are not acclimated to the new culture around them and need help to navigate their new life. While much of the helpful information is located online, there are too many sites to research, making this important part of being an international student become messy and a chore. To counter this, we created UNIversal, a GIANT hub that helps immigrant and international students learn about their new country and school. It contains a plethora of information encompassing what these students need to survive and thrive. It is our mission to help international students find housing, understand the new country's language(s), and find job opportunities and extracurriculars.
